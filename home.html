Highlights of the club

The Eduvos Coding Club is one vibrant community of coders, innovators, and problem-solvers. Some of our responsibilities include hosting coding challenges, workshops, and hackathons, where members can showcase their skills, learn from industry experts, and network with like-minded individuals. Our offers range from beginer friendly tutorials to advanced project developments to help with the growth of our members.
